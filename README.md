# Life International Pharmaceutical Company (LIPC)

## Overview
Life International Pharmaceutical Company (LIPC) is an upcoming state-of-the-art medical, scientific, and pharmaceutical facility based in Kampala, Uganda. Its mission is to advance healthcare innovation, research, and sustainable production for Africa and the global community.

LIPC will host three core divisions:
- *Scientific Research Laboratory*
- *Pharmaceutical Manufacturing Plant*
- *Advanced Hospital & Clinical Facility*

## Mission
To provide life-saving innovation, scientific excellence, and high-quality pharmaceutical products that strengthen healthcare across Africa.

## Vision
A healthier, self-reliant Africa empowered by world-class medical research, responsible pharmaceutical manufacturing, and breakthrough innovation.
