# Life International Pharmaceutical Company (LIPC)

The *Life International Pharmaceutical Company (LIPC)* is an upcoming state-of-the-art pharmaceutical, research, and clinical facility being developed in Kampala, Uganda. LIPC is designed to become a leading center of scientific excellence, advancing healthcare access across East and Central Africa through innovation, high-quality medical production, and world-class research.
