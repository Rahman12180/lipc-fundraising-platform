Life International Pharmaceutical Company (LIPC) — Business Plan

Prepared by: Dr. Katabazi Abdallah, PhD


---

1. Executive Summary

Life International Pharmaceutical Company (LIPC) is a large-scale pharmaceutical, research, and clinical facility designed to transform healthcare across East and Central Africa. Built on a one-square-mile scientific campus, LIPC will host advanced laboratories, clean rooms, manufacturing plants, a hospital facility, and research centres.

LIPC will produce essential medicines, laboratory reagents, and critical medical water solutions such as D5, D10, and Normal Saline (NS) — products that are frequently in shortage in Africa.

The total project cost is USD 167 million.

4. Products & Services

A. Medical Water Production

LIPC will manufacture hospital-grade IV medical waters including:

D5 (5% Dextrose Water) – hydration, energy supply, pediatric use

D10 (10% Dextrose Water) – hypoglycemia management, emergency care

NS (0.9% Normal Saline) – surgical, emergency, and inpatient care

Sterile Water for Injection – research and clinical formulations


Medical water is one of the most urgently needed hospital products across Africa.

6. Market Analysis

The region depends heavily on imports for medicines, medical water, and sterile supplies. Hospitals frequently face shortages of NS, D5, D10, antibiotics, and laboratory reagents.

LIPC will fill these gaps and become a major supplier to:

Government hospitals

Private clinics

NGOs and humanitarian agencies

Laboratories and universities

Regional medical distributors
