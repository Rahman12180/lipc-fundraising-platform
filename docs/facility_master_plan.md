1. Overview

The Life International Pharmaceutical Company (LIPC) Master Plan outlines the long-term physical development of the facility, covering land requirements, infrastructure layout, building functions, and future expansion phases. The goal is to establish a world-class integrated pharmaceutical, medical manufacturing, and research ecosystem serving East and Central Africa.
